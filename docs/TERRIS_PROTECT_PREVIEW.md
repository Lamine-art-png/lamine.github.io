# Terris Protect Preview

Terris Protect is staged as a preview boundary only.

Future event types may include:
- pest risk alert
- disease risk alert
- spray window candidate
- spray task
- spray application reported
- crop-protection observation

Current limitations:
- no chemical prescription
- no pesticide recommendation
- no regulatory claim
- no clickable dead workflow
- no autonomous action

Required future inputs include local pest and disease observations, crop context, weather context, label-safe constraints, advisor review, and evidence references.
