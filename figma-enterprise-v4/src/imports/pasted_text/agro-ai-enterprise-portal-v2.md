Refine Version 1. Do not create a new concept. Improve the existing AGRO-AI Enterprise Portal design so it feels more premium, sleeker, more minimalist, more magical, and more like a serious AI-native enterprise workspace.

This is still NOT a marketing site. It is a functional product interface. Prioritize work, clarity, operational control, and trust.

The current version is slightly better, but it still needs to feel more like a high-end product used by serious enterprise teams, closer to Claude, ChatGPT, Linear, Stripe Dashboard, Vercel, Ramp, and Palantir-style operational software.

Design direction:
- More premium
- More minimalist
- More functional
- More spacious but not empty
- More refined typography
- Less dashboard-template feeling
- Less generic SaaS
- Less marketing
- More “real work happens here”
- More AI-native, but without sci-fi effects
- Calm, powerful, serious, corporate, expensive

Do not add clutter.
Do not add big decorative illustrations.
Do not add random charts.
Do not use fake marketing claims.
Do not add giant hero sections.
Do not use floating right rails.
Do not overlap content.
Do not show internal IDs.
Do not use “certified”, “fully compliant”, “regulator approved”, or any fake compliance language.
Do not make it playful.
