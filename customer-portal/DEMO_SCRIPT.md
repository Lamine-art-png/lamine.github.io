# AGRO-AI Water Command Center Walkthrough Script

Use this script for customer, integration partner, investor, OEM, and strategic partner walkthroughs of `https://app.agroai-pilot.com`.

## Five Minute Flow

1. **Open the portal** at `https://app.agroai-pilot.com`.
   - Position AGRO-AI as the Water Command Center: scattered signals become reconciled recommendations, execution tasks, verification evidence, and reporting.
2. **Open the evaluation workspace** with **Open Water Command Center**.
   - The Command page opens with the **Representative data** package preloaded and a verified decision already on screen — no setup required before showing value.
3. **Introduce the main story**.
   - Use the hero line: **Scattered irrigation data becomes a verified water decision.**
   - The header shows the title, subtitle, and the status row: *Evaluation workspace · Mixed sources · Evidence chain active · Backend intelligence online.*
4. **Read the executive strip**.
   - Current decision, confidence, evidence completeness, and estimated water savings are visible above the fold.
5. **Walk the decision operations canvas**.
   - Left: **Source intelligence** rows (controller history, weather, soil, flow, observation, earth observation, uploaded records).
   - Middle: **Decision pipeline** — Sources, Normalize, Reconcile, Decide, Verify.
   - Right: **Verified water decision** with crop, block, driver, confidence, evidence, and verification.
6. **Refresh intelligence**.
   - Click **Refresh intelligence**. The pipeline stages animate; expand **Analysis trace** to show the structured operational sequence.
   - When the backend is reachable the decision updates from the Workbench result; otherwise representative-data analysis remains available.
7. **Switch workspace scenarios**.
   - Use the workspace switcher near the title: Alpha Vineyard, Almond Orchard, Multi-Farm Portfolio, Partner Data Validation. Each reloads representative records and updates the decision, reconciliation, and report.
8. **Add or manage sources**.
   - Click **Add or manage sources** in the Source intelligence header to open the right-side drawer: Connected systems, Upload records, API access, Partner feeds. Upload a CSV and choose **Analyze uploaded records**.
9. **Move the Evidence chain**.
   - In the decision panel: **Approve schedule**, **Confirm applied water**, **Add field observation**, **Verify outcome** — each updates the evidence chain.
10. **Open the Executive report preview**.
   - Show farm, block, recommendation, planned vs applied water, variance, evidence completeness, estimated water savings, and verification status.
   - Use **Export CSV**, **Preview report**, or **Print report**.
11. **Show Integrations**.
   - Open **Request backend setup** for WiseConn or Talgil. Copy or download the setup brief and point to the audit event.

## 30 Second Version

1. Open Water Command Center — a verified decision is already on screen.
2. Read the executive strip and the verified water decision.
3. Click **Refresh intelligence**; expand the Analysis trace.
4. Switch a workspace scenario from the title switcher.
5. Open **Add or manage sources** and show the drawer tabs.
6. Move the Evidence chain and preview the report.
7. Open Integrations and prepare a backend setup request.

## Talk Track

AGRO-AI reads controller history, weather, soil context, field observations, uploaded records, flow evidence, crop context, water cost context, and earth-observation sample layers, then turns them into a verified water decision that a farm team can schedule, verify, and report.

## Current Limitation Language

Provider credentials must be stored server-side through a credential vault and tenant provisioning flow. The static portal does not store provider secrets in browser storage, and the earth-observation layer shown here is a sample layer rather than a live partner integration.
